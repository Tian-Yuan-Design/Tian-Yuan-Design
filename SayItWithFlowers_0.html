Inspiration is the acient game that two people hold the can at two spots and only a string connect the two cans, one speaks toward his can, another one can hear his voice from can through string. It's amazing as "I know you without seeing you". So we want t create the anarchic communication experience via giving participants most space and freedom.

N.N. (ca. 1889): Tin can telephone. https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Trådtelefon-illustration.png

In our concept, we need flowers as receiver. To add uncertainty, enjoyment and surprise to product, we choose random flower to playback. So what you hear now maybe is recorded much time ago in thousands meters away!

Below is the state flow of this concept.

· How to make a flower? ·

Occasionally, we know "Dusyma Knüpferli" from a material workshop. Dusyma Knüpferli can be bent and connected so that we can use thousands of same-shape units to create a huge organic shape.

· Make of Dusyma Knüpferli, Arduino, PurrData, APP Inventor ·

To make one flower, we need:

Electric Parts

1 MG996-R Servo
1 Adafruit NeoPixel 12 LED Ring
1 Electret Microphone
1 Android Phone
1 ESP-12N Board
1 Infrared Distance Sensor

Mechanical Parts

3 80x80cm Paper
500 Dusyma Knüpferli
1 40x40cm 3mm Acrylic
9 3mm Diameter Nuts
Various scraps of Acrylic and Elastics

The flower is close and "breathing" when no people near it. When the infrared distance sensor detect people enter the detectable area, arduino will command servo to rotate 90 degree to make the flower open.

· Technical Details ·

All tools we use contain, Arduino, PurrData, APP Inventor, Node.js. We drew "state machine diagram" and "sequence diagram" firstly to make our mindset clear.

Arduino needs to receive data from sensor and PurrData to chage the flower's status: idle, is recording, is playing. Each status have different changing lights and movement.

Calling Different Functions

PurrData needs to record all flowers and phones status, and communicate with Arduino, APP Inventor and Node.js to switch flowers and phones' status in time.
