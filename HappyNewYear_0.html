· An Easy-to-Play and Joyful Journey ·

Press "SPACE" to JUMP, press "FORWARD/BACKWARD" to RUN in left/right direction. Please collect coin and food, avoid naughty boys and firecrackers. If you can find dragon before the end, you can drive it and fly to the end directly.

In this game, you have to keep your life not less than 1, and arrive the end in 1 minute. After getting the end and driving the dragon, you may unlock next city(according to your lucky money amount).

In various cities, you will meet different game element. For example, in Beijing(China), we put dumplings, firecrackers, Beijing quadrangle(SiHeYuan) in scen, in Ho Chi Minh City(Vietnam), we put ZongZi, peach tree, leaves in scen.
